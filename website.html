Dinosaur of the Week:

Hadrosaurous

The Hadrosaurous, found in NJ in 1838, kicked off our fascination with dinosaurs. William Foulke, on his vacation in Haddonsfield, was the first to unearth an almost complete skeleton of a dinosaur after learning that workers had found gigantic bones while mining. Visitors can still climb down into the marl pit today, and experience the primordial, other worldy quiet that echoes inside the walls.
